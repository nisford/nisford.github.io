We Offer A wide range of Services To Keep you rolling

We offer the following indepth services

Mechanical work

We understand the unique needs of Nissan and Ford vehicles, and we use state-of-the-art equipment and genuine parts to ensure that your car receives the care it deserves. From minor tune-ups to major overhauls, trust us to keep your Nissan or Ford running smoothly and reliably for miles to come. Visit us today and experience the difference!

Panel Beating

We understand that accidents happen, but that doesn't mean your vehicle has to suffer. Our skilled panel beaters are here to restore your Nissan or Ford to its pristine condition, using advanced techniques and equipment to repair dents, scratches, and damage of all kinds. From straightening frames to seamlessly blending paint, our goal is to make your Nissan or Ford look as good as new.

Auto electrical

We provide top-quality auto-electrical repair services specifically tailored for Nissans and Fords. From diagnosing pesky electrical glitches to fixing faulty wiring, our experienced technicians have the knowledge and skills to handle any electrical issue your vehicle may encounter.

Diagnostics

Our state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment allows us to pinpoint problems quickly and accurately, saving you time and money. Once we've diagnosed the issue, we'll work closely with you to ensure you understand the necessary repairs and their importance.

Service Plans

We're dedicated to ensuring your Nissan or Ford stays in peak condition for kilometres to come. Our comprehensive service and maintenance offerings cover everything your vehicle needs to perform at its best, from routine check-ups to preventative maintenance.
